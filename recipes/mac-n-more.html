Mac n More

Description

Loaded mac and cheese features creamy elbow macaroni in a rich cheddar and mozzarella sauce, topped with crispy breadcrumbs, crumbled bacon, and fresh green onions. It's a hearty and indulgent comfort food perfect for family meals or gatherings.

Preparation Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 35 mins Serves: 4-6

Ingredients

  • Mac and Cheese

    • 8 oz elbow macaroni
    • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
    • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
    • 2 cups milk
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/2 tsp onion powder
    • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
    • salt and pepper to taste

  • Toppings

    • 4 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
    • 1/2 cup sausage, cooked and crumbled*
    • 1/2 cup green onions, sliced
    • 1/2 cup breadcrumps (panko*)
    • 2 tbsp melted butter
    • 1/4 grated parmesan cheese
* - optional ingredient

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Cook the macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Cheese Sauce

    In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for about 1 minute to make a roux. Gradually whisk in the milk, cooking until the mixture thickens and starts to bubble. Reduce heat to low and stir in cheddar and mozzarella cheeses until melted and smooth. Add garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  3. Combine

    Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, stirring until well coated.

  4. Add Toppings

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Transfer the mac and cheese to a baking dish. Top with crumbled bacon, sausage (if using), and sliced green onions. Mix breadcrumbs with melted butter and sprinkle over the top of the mac and cheese. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

  5. Bake

    Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and crispy.

  6. Serve

    Let the mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
